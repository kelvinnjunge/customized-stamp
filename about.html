How much do rubber stamps cost in Kenya?
Rubber stamps in Kenya start from KSh 800 for small self-inking stamps. Medium stamps cost KSh 1,000, large stamps KSh 1,500, dater stamps KSh 1,500, and company seals from KSh 1,200. All prices include free design and one free revision. See the full price list →
How fast can I get a rubber stamp in Nairobi?
Most stamps and company seals are produced same-day in Nairobi. Orders placed before noon can be delivered the same day within Nairobi. Heavy-duty stamps and seals may take 24–48 hours.
Can I pay with M-Pesa for my rubber stamp?
Yes! We accept M-Pesa, bank transfer, and cash for all rubber stamps and company seals in Kenya. A 50% deposit is accepted for larger or bulk orders.
Do you deliver rubber stamps outside Nairobi?
Yes, we deliver rubber stamps and company seals nationwide across Kenya via G4S courier and other services. We deliver to Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, Eldoret, Thika, Nyeri, and all other towns. Delivery takes 1–3 business days.
Are your company seals accepted by Kenya banks and KRA?
Yes. Our company seals are accepted by all Kenya banks, KRA, NHIF, NSSF, and all government institutions. We make them to the standard specifications required by Kenya law.
Do I need to send you my design or logo?
No design experience needed! Simply tell us your text, company name, and any requirements. We create a free digital design proof and send it to you for approval before production. You can request as many revisions as needed at no charge.
What ink colours are available for rubber stamps?
We offer rubber stamps in blue, black, red, green, and violet ink. Replacement ink pads are also available for all stamp models from KSh 300.
