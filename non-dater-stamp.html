FAQs

Non-Dater Stamp Kenya — Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a non-dater stamp cost in Kenya?
Non-dater stamps start from KSh 800 (small), KSh 1,000 (medium), and KSh 1,500 (large). All include free design.
What is the difference between a dater and non-dater stamp?
A dater stamp has an adjustable date. A non-dater has only text, logos, or signatures — no date. Non-dater stamps are more affordable.
Can I get a non-dater stamp with my company logo?
Yes. Send your logo on WhatsApp and we'll design and produce your stamp within hours.
What sizes do non-dater stamps come in?
Small (38×14mm), medium (47×18mm), large (69×47mm), and round (42mm diameter). Custom sizes available.
How fast can I get a non-dater stamp in Nairobi?
Same-day. Most non-dater stamps are produced and delivered within Nairobi for orders placed before noon.
